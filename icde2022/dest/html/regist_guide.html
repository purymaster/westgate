Registration Guideline

Registration Process

Step 1 Click the “Register now” button below
Step 2 Fill out the participant information
Step 3 Choose your category
Step 4 Choose payment method and make payment
Step 5 Check your email for payment confirmation (Successfully completed)
  • If you do not receive the confirmation letter via email, please contact the Secretariat (icde2022.info@gmail.com).
  • The on-line registration page is optimized for Chrome and Microsoft Edge. If you have any problems with the registration, please download the registration form and send it back to the Secretariat by email(icde2022.info@gmail.com)
