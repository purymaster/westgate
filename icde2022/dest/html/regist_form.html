ICDE Leadership Summit 2022 Secretariat collects and manages personal information within the minimum extent in order to arrange our events.

The items of personal information to be collected and the purposes are as follows :

1. Items of personal information to be collected Name, organization name(department, professional title), nationality, contact details(mobile phone, email address, etc.) (The owner of information has a right to reject to give his/her personal information. However, the owner of information may not be able to participate in the events, if he/she does not provide us with essential information as above.)

2. Purposes for which personal information is collected and used The aforementioned items would be used for confirming the participation in the events, authentication process to verify identification, appropriate preparation to proceed with successful event progress, and follow-up management (Response on questions from participants regarding the events, sending a letter of appreciation, providing further information in relation with the events, and etc.)

3. Period for which personal information is held and used In principle, personal information would be discarded as soon as the purpose of collecting information is attained.
