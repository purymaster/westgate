COVID-19 Info for prospective participants

Entry and quarantine procedure in the context of COVID-19

  1. Step1 You are required to have a diplomatic passport or A-2 (Government Official) visa to be exempted from the mandatory 2-week quarantine for inbound travelers to Korea. Kindly be advised to start your visa application process as soon as possible and contact a Korean diplomatic mission in your country for more details. Should there be any issue in this regard, please inform the IPS Secretariat at
    [email] knpcooperation@police.go.kr,
    [phone] +82-2-3150-2280, +82-2-3150-2880.
  2. Step2 When you enter Korea, you must carry and submit the original copy of your 'PCR-negative certificate' issued in advance by a medical organization acknowledged by the Korean missions in your home country. The list of the medical organization for the PCR test is posted on each embassy's website. The certificates are valid, only if issued within 72 hours before your departure time.
  3. Step3 Upon entering Korea, you must undergo another PCR test and wait for the result in a designated accommodation around the airport. This process will take approximately 9 to 16 hours depending on your arrival time. If your flight arrives in the afternoon, you are advised to come to Korea on 19th September as the waiting time could be longer.
  4. Step4 All entrants exempted from the quarantine must install a 'self-diagnosis mobile application' on their mobile devices for reinforced active monitoring by the Ministry of Health and Welfare. The application is for you to report any COVID-19 symptoms voluntarily once a day during your stay in Korea. More information and installation link are available at http://ncov.mohw.go.kr/selfcheck.

2021 IPS anti-infection and prevention guideline

Measures for anti-infection and prevention will be put in place in compliance with the related policies and guidelines of the Korean public health authorities.

  1. Step1 A designated person responsible for disease control and prevention will stay on the event site. The IPS venue will be sanitized regularly as well as before and after the event.
  2. Step2 You must wear a face mask all the time when you are out of your hotel room or a private vehicle. If you have any symptoms of COVID-19, such as fever, respiratory symptoms, cough, sore throats, etc., you should stay in your accommodation and inform the IPS Secretariat immediately for further steps. Please note that the host is not responsible for any medical costs of the participants, including expenses for treating COVID-19. You are required to buy appropriate travel insurance and upload a copy of the insurance certificate on MY PAGE of this website before your travel.
  3. Step3 Before you enter the event venue, your body temperature will be checked each time. If the temperature is higher than 37.5 degrees Celcius, you will not be allowed to attend the event.
  4. Step4 The IPS Secretariat is collecting personal information of the participants to submit a participant list to Korean public health authorities. Kindly upload a copy of the information page of your passport, as the list should include your passport name exactly. The information will not be used for any other reasons except facilitating the 2021 IPS.
