Dear police leaders and colleagues around the world,

Please accept my sincerest appreciation and warmest welcome for you to come to the International Police Summit (IPS) in Seoul, the Republic of Korea, from 20th to 23rd October 2021. It is my greatest honor to invite you to this unique event hosted by the Korean National Police Agency to share a common understanding of the need for global partnership in policing. Primarily, the Summit will provide a meaningful forum for police leaders worldwide to resume international exchanges and expand cooperation to cope with new threats in the new era where we have to live with COVID-19.

Notably, the virus has caused rapid changes in many aspects of our lives. The police worldwide are facing new challenges in the global crisis with the advent of the new normal. In times of upheaval, we have to find a new approach to police activities, which must be supported by sharing, solidarity, and cooperation at the global level. I believe the 2021 IPS will be an excellent opportunity for all participants for sustainable communication and friendship.

The Republic of Korea will celebrate its 76th National Police Day during the event. The Korean National Police will host other online and offline events for the same week of the 2021 IPS, including the Korean Police World Expo, the International Symposium on Cybercrime Response, and the International CSI Conference and Exhibition. The participants in the 2021 IPS will also have the opportunity to attend these events.

Ensuring continuous commitment of the Korean National Police working together with you for world peace and safety, I look forward to meeting you all soon.

Commissioner General
Korean National Police Agency
Kim Chang-Yong
