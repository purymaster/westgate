International Symposium on Cybercrime Response 2021 (ISCR 2021)

The Korean National Police Agency(KNPA) has been annually hosting the International Symposium on Cybercrime Response(ISCR) as a part of its effort to combat borderless cybercrime. Since its initiation back in 2000, the ISCR has now established itself as one of the largest law enforcement symposiums in the area of cyber security. Mainly due to the outbreak of COVID-19 across the world, the ISCR 2021 will be operated in hybrid(online and offline) basis. All contents will be available from October 18th to 22nd, at the official website(https://iscr.cyber.go.kr/eng). Also the symposium will be held on-site(Four Seasons Hotel in Seoul, Korea) from October 18th to 19th. Across the globe, addressing growing and evolving cybercrime amid these difficult times has become a major concern for law enforcement agencies and their partners. The KNPA and ISCR Secretariat deeply believe that the cooperation between multiple law enforcement agencies, as well as the active involvement of private sector and academia will strengthen us all in the fight against cybercrime.

Official website : https://iscr.cyber.go.kr/eng

CSI KOREA 2021

The 7th International CSI Conference will be held from October 20 to 22, 2021, as a webinar through the video conference system. This year, the agenda of the Conference is "Innovation in Forensic Science : From the Crime Scene to the Court". "CSI KOREA" has been annually held since 2015, co-hosted by Korean National Police Agency & Korean Association of CSI. It is now the largest forensic science conference in Korea. It will be a meaningful occasion for scholars and researchers to share major research results and up-to-date study trends in both Korea and overseas. This year, the Conference will provide better opportunities for experts to interact and develop a network to meet the expectation of forensic investigators.

Official website : https://www.kcsi.go.kr/

Korea Police World Expo 2021

Korea Police World Expo (KPEX) introduces Smart Policing Technology that will lead the revitalization of the security industry through the core technology of the Fourth Industrial Revolution. The need is increasing day by day for the development of high-tech police security systems, scientific investigation techniques, and related technologies to cope with various evolving and diversifying crimes. In response, KPEX aims to promote the K-Cop Wave Program around the world and strengthen the international security cooperation network, bringing together high-tech security industry, police equipment and solutions for business exchanges. The 3rd KPEX will be held from October 20 to 23 in Songdo ConvensiA, Incheon, Korea.

※ The Expo is open from 9:00 to 17:00 (Last entrance : 16:30)

Official website : https://eng.police-expo.com/
